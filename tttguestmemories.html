How to Use This Page

1. View Your Keepsake Collection

All guest memories submitted through Guest Memories automatically appear inside your Keepsake Box.

2. Browse Memory Folders

Each guest is organized into a dedicated folder, making memories easy to find and revisit.

3. Expand Guest Folders

Click a folder to reveal the memories, messages, and photos submitted by that guest.

4. Read Personal Messages

Enjoy heartfelt wishes, stories, reflections, and memories shared by family and friends.

5. View Shared Photos

Browse photos submitted by guests and relive meaningful moments from your wedding celebration.

6. Open Photos Full Screen

Click any photo to view it in a larger format and enjoy every detail.

7. Explore Guest Contributions

See what each guest contributed and appreciate their unique perspective on your wedding day.

8. Memories Stay Organized

Messages and photos are grouped automatically, creating a structured and easy-to-navigate archive.

9. Revisit Special Moments

Return anytime to relive the memories shared by your guests.

10. Build Your Wedding Story

Together, guest messages and photos create a lasting record of your wedding experience.

11. Create a Lasting Keepsake

Your Keepsake Box becomes a digital treasure chest of memories that can be enjoyed for years.

12. Preserve Your Wedding Legacy

Capture not only what happened on your wedding day, but how it was experienced by the people who shared it with you.

Important Notes

i
Guest Memories submissions automatically appear inside Keepsake Box without any additional action.
i
Each guest receives a dedicated folder that groups their messages and photos together.
i
Keepsake Box is designed for viewing and preserving memories, while Guest Memories is used to collect them.
i
Your Keepsake Box becomes more meaningful over time as additional memories and photos are collected.

In short: Keepsake Box transforms guest messages and photos into a beautiful wedding memory archive, allowing you to revisit special moments, explore shared experiences, and preserve your wedding story for years to come.
