Guest Memories & Messages is where your guests’ photos and notes come together after the wedding.

Every message and image shared by your guests is saved here — organized and easy to explore anytime.

How It Works

Using These Memories

You can copy any photos or messages shared by your guests and reuse them in your “Just Married” album.

It’s a simple way to turn guest moments into a beautiful post-wedding story on your website.

Why It Matters

Your guests see the wedding through their own eyes. This archive lets you relive those moments — candid, emotional, and completely unique.
