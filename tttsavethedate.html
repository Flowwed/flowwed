Save the Date Manager

i
The Save the Date Manager page allows you to manage and send your Save the Date announcement to guests before the official RSVP stage. Once the announcement video is ready, you can preview it, choose recipients, and send it by email or SMS.

How to Use This Page

1. Preview Your Save the Date Video

The video preview shows the Save the Date announcement your guests will receive.

2. Select Guests

Use the checkboxes to select individual guests or use the master checkbox to select the full list at once.

3. Send by Email

Click Send Email to open the message composer. The Save The Date link is added automatically, so guests can open it immidiately.

4. Notify by SMS

Use Send SMS to generate a QR code and quickly share the link from your phone.

Important Notes

i
Save the Date announcements are typically sent before RSVP invitations to help guests reserve the date in advance.
i
Always preview the announcement video before sending it to guests.
