The Our Story page lets you create a beautiful relationship timeline for your wedding website. Write your story in up to five sections, upload photos, add notes to your images, preview the page your guests will see, and share the journey that brought you together.

How to Use This Page

1. Enable Your Story Page

Use the page visibility option to show or hide your Our Story page from guests. Keep it hidden while editing, then enable it when your story is ready.

2. Build Story Sections

Create up to five story sections to describe your journey together, from how you met to the beginning of your new chapter.

3. Add Section Titles

Give each section a meaningful title, such as “How We Met,” “The Proposal,” or any custom chapter that fits your relationship.

4. Write Your Story

Use the text editor to write your story. You can update each section whenever you want.

5. Upload Photos

Add photos to your Our Story page. You can upload up to 35 photos, with each photo limited to 5MB.

6. Choose Photo Slots

Select the photo slot before uploading. Photo 1 is used as the top cover photo, while the remaining photos appear in the story gallery.

7. Add Photo Notes

After uploading a photo, you can add a short note or caption to explain the memory, place, or moment behind the image.

8. Review Your Gallery

Use the gallery area to review uploaded photos, captions, and the visual flow of your Our Story page.

9. Save Text Changes

Click Save Text Changes after editing your story sections. This keeps your latest text updates safely stored.

10. Preview Guest View

Use Preview Your Page to see exactly how your Our Story page will appear to wedding guests.

11. Send Updates

If you want to notify guests about your updated story page, use the update option to open the guest communication tool.

Important Notes

i
Photo 1 is used as the top cover photo, so choose a strong image for that slot.
i
Always save your text changes before leaving the page.
i
Guests will only see the Our Story page if the webpage is enabled.
