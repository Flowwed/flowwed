The RSVP Manager page allows you to send the RSVP form directly to your guests. Once your RSVP cinematic invitation is ready, you can preview it, choose recipients, and send it by email or SMS.

How to Use This Page

1. Check the Invitation Video

The video preview shows the cinematic invitation your guests will receive, along with the RSVP form they will be asked to complete.

2. Complete Wedding Details

Fill in the blue fields: wedding date, time, venue, address, and RSVP deadline. These details will appear on the RSVP form your guests receive.

3. Enable Meal Selection

Turn on meal selection if you want guests to choose food options such as chicken, fish, beef, vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or custom meals.

4. Set Guest Limit

Choose whether each RSVP is limited to one guest, up to two guests, or more than two guests.

5. Preview the Guest Form

The preview section shows how the RSVP form will look for guests, including attendance, guest count, meal choice, email, and message fields.

6. Select Guests

Use the checkboxes to select individual guests or use the master checkbox to select the full list at once.

7. Send by Email

Click Send Email to open the message composer and send your RSVP announcement directly from this page. The RSVP video link is included automatically, allowing guests to access it right away and respond.

8. Notify by SMS

Use Send SMS to generate a QR code and quickly share the link from your phone.

9. Track Responses

After guests respond, their answers are saved automatically. You can review RSVP status, attendance, meal choices, and messages in RSVP Reports.

Important Notes

i
The section marked “This section is for you to fill out” is only for the couple or event organizer. Guests will not see that editing area.
i
Use the RSVP Reports shortcut or visit the RSVP Reports page to review guest responses and attendance status.
i
Always check the preview before sending invitations. It helps you make sure the date, time, venue, address, deadline, meal options, and guest limits are correct.
