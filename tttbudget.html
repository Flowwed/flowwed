The Wedding Budget page is your financial control center. It helps you plan, distribute, and track every dollar — while giving you a clear picture of how healthy your budget really is.

This tool is designed to feel light and elegant, but under the surface it’s a real planning system that reacts instantly to every change you make.

How to Use It

Set Total Budget
Start at the top. Enter your overall wedding budget — everything else adapts to this number.
Allocate by Category
Assign budgets for Venue, Catering, Decor, and more. Each category becomes its own mini-budget.
Add Expenses
Open a category and add real costs as they happen. Totals update instantly.
Track Progress
Progress bars and remaining values show you exactly how close each category is to its limit.
Budget Health
The top indicator shows whether you’re safe, approaching the limit, or going over budget.
Export & Print
Download a full CSV report of your budget and expenses — perfect for sharing or printing.

Why It Works

This budget is not just a list — it is a living system. Every number is connected: categories affect totals, expenses affect health, and your overall plan stays visible at all times.

You always know: how much you planned, how much you spent, how much remains — and whether your wedding is financially on track.

The goal is simple: confidence instead of stress. You make decisions calmly, knowing exactly where you stand.
