Venue & Map helps your guests know exactly where and when your wedding takes place. Here you set the location, date, and time of your event.

Once configured, guests can open the map, get directions, and plan their arrival with confidence.

How to Set It Up

Enter Address
Type your venue location.
Generate Map
Create a live map link.
Select Date
Choose the wedding day.
Set Time
Define when guests should arrive.
Save Changes
Store everything securely.

Why It Matters

Clear directions prevent confusion and late arrivals. Your guests will feel calm and prepared, knowing exactly where to go and when to be there.
