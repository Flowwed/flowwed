The Travel & Event page is where you build the full logistics and experience for your wedding guests. Everything you enter here will appear on your public wedding page.

This is your control center for timing, travel details, and venue information — helping guests understand exactly how to plan their trip.

How This Page Works

Build Schedule
Add ceremony and event timeline for your guests.
Travel & Stay
Provide airport info, transfers, and hotel options.
Set Venue
Enter address — map link is created automatically.
Add Details
Include dress code, weather, and extra guidance.
Create FAQ
Answer common guest questions in advance.
Preview Page
See exactly what your guests will experience.
Auto-Save & Updates
All changes are saved and updated instatly for your guests in real time.

What This Controls

All information here feeds directly into your wedding website. Guests will use this page to plan their arrival, understand your schedule, and navigate to your venue.

The venue address automatically generates a live map link, so guests can open directions instantly.

Travel & Event ensures your guests feel prepared, informed, and confident about every step of your celebration.
