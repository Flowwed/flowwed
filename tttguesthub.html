Welcome to your Guest Hub page — the heart of your guest experience.

All you need to do here is create your guest list. Add guests, edit their details directly in the table, and track RSVP responses.

Your Guest Hub is seamlessly synced across the rest of your dashboard, allowing you to manage further communication with your guests — from sending updates and Save the Dates to collecting RSVPs and sharing wedding details via email and SMS.

How This Page Works

The Guest Hub is designed to feel calm and powerful at the same time. All edits are saved automatically. There is no “Save” button — the system works for you in real time.

Add Guests
Use the form to add new guests with name, email, and phone. Each guest is saved instantly.
Guest List
Your full guest list appears in the table. Click any cell to edit it directly. Changes are saved automatically.
Filters
Filter your guest list by RSVP status: accepted, declined, maybe, or no response.
Send Messages
Select guests and click “Send Email & SMS” to compose your message. Messages are sent with progress updates.
Download & Print
Download your guest list as an Excel file for printing or sharing.

Guest Hub is built to remove stress from planning. It keeps your guest list clear, your communication smooth, and your wedding beautifully organized — so you can focus on what truly matters.
