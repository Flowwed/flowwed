Guest Hub

i
Guest Hub is the central database for your wedding guests. Add, organize, edit, and manage guest information from one place.
All you need to do here is build your guest list.

Your Guest Hub is automatically connected to the rest of your wedding dashboard, so once your guests are added, you won't need to enter their information again.

You can then use the same guest list to send Save the Dates, share wedding updates, collect RSVPs, create seating charts, request guest memories, and communicate through email or SMS from anywhere in your dashboard.

How to Use This Page

1. Build Your Guest List

Enter each guest's name, email address, and optional phone number to add them to your guest database.

2. Edit Guest Information

Update names, emails, and phone numbers directly inside the guest table whenever information changes.

3. Export Guest Data

Download and print your guest list as a CSV file for planners, vendors, or offline records.

4. Remove Guests

Delete guests that are no longer needed while keeping your guest list clean and accurate.

5. Check RSVP Reports

Use the RSVP Reports shortcut or visit the RSVP Reports page to review guest responses and attendance information.

Important Notes

i
Keep guest information updated to make sure emails, SMS messages, RSVPs, and reports work correctly.
i
Deleting a guest may affect how that guest appears in other connected dashboard pages.
