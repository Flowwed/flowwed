1. What is FloWWeds?
FloWWeds creates cinematic wedding invitations powered by AI, turning your photos into a film-style experience.
2. How long does it take?
Most invitations are ready within 7–10 days after you submit your photos and details.
3. How many photos do I need?
Typically 6–10 photos work perfectly, but even fewer can still create a beautiful result.
4. Can I choose the cinematic style?
Yes. You can choose the style that best fits your wedding theme and personality.
5. Do guests need to install anything?
No. Guests simply open a link and watch your invitation instantly.
6. Can I share it by phone?
Yes. You can send your invitation via text, WhatsApp, email, or any messaging app.
7. Is this better than printed invitations?
It’s faster, more memorable, and often more affordable — plus guests never lose it.
8. Can I edit it later?
Yes. You can update details or information anytime through your control panel.
9. Do I get a wedding website too?
Yes. Every package includes a beautiful wedding website.
10. Is it private?
Yes. Only people with your link can view your invitation.
