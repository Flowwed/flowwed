1. What is FloWWeds?
FloWWeds creates cinematic AI-powered wedding invitations and interactive wedding websites that feel like a personalized digital experience instead of a traditional invite.
2. How does it work?
You send your photos, wedding details, and preferences — we transform them into a cinematic invitation and interactive wedding experience your guests can open instantly from any device.
3. How long does it take to create our invitation?
Most projects are completed within 7–10 days depending on customization and content.
4. Do guests need to install an app?
No. Guests simply tap your private link and everything opens instantly in their browser.
5. Will it work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. FloWWeds is designed to work smoothly across iPhone, Android, tablets, and desktop devices.
6. Can older guests use it easily?
Yes. The experience is intentionally designed to feel simple, intuitive, and easy for guests of all ages.

Yes. You can customize photos, videos, colors, text, music, event details, RSVP settings, and more.

Absolutely. You can choose from different visual moods and styles to match your wedding aesthetic and personality.
