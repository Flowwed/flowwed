Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is FloWWeds?
FloWWeds creates cinematic wedding invitations and interactive wedding websites designed to feel like a personalized digital experience rather than a traditional invitation.

Every experience is crafted by real people, with AI used as a creative tool to help bring your story to life.
2. How does it work?
You send us your photos and wedding details, and we transform them into a cinematic invitation and interactive wedding experience your guests can open instantly on any device.
3. How long does it take to create our invitation?
Most projects are completed within 10 days, depending on customization and content.
4. Do guests need to install an app?
No. Guests simply open your private link, and everything loads instantly in their browser.
5. Will it work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. FloWWeds is designed to work smoothly across iPhone, Android, tablets, and desktop devices.
6. Can older guests use it easily?
Yes. The experience is intentionally designed to feel simple, intuitive, and accessible for guests of all ages.
7. Can we personalize every section?
Yes. You can customize photos, videos, text, music, event details, RSVP settings, and more.
8. Can we start now and upload photos later?
Yes. You can reserve your experience and complete your photo uploads later when you're ready.
9. What happens after we place our order?
After your order is placed, we’ll guide you step by step through photo uploads, wedding details, and personalization. You’ll receive clear instructions and be able to review everything before delivery.
10. Can we add music to our wedding gallery?
Yes. Your personal wedding galleries and memories can include music.
11. Can guests RSVP directly from the invitation?
Yes. Guests can RSVP directly through your wedding experience without needing separate forms or apps.
12. Can guests share their memories?
Yes. Guests can upload memories, including up to 5 photos and personal messages, directly through your wedding platform.
13. Can we update information after sending the invitation?
Yes. You can update schedules, locations, travel details, and other information anytime.
14. Do you support multiple wedding events?
Yes. You can include rehearsal dinners, after-parties, brunches, ceremonies, receptions, and more.
15. Will guests receive directions and travel information?
Yes. You can include maps, hotel details, schedules, transportation information, and event guidance.
16. Can we include a wedding gallery?
Yes. Your experience can include galleries for engagement photos, wedding memories, and guest uploads.
17. Is my personal information secure?
Your privacy is important to us. We do not sell, publish, or share your personal information with third parties.

You can review our Privacy Policy here .
18. Are our photos and videos stored securely?
Yes. We use secure cloud storage and protected access systems to keep your content safe.
19. Can we share it through text or WhatsApp?
Yes. Your invitation can be shared through text message, WhatsApp, email, Instagram, or any messaging platform.
20. Do we need professional photos?
No. Even high-quality phone photos can help us create a beautiful cinematic experience.
21. How many photos should we upload?
We will need 8 photos of the bride and groom, following a few simple guidelines you’ll receive after placing your order.
22. Can we preview everything before guests see it?
Yes. You’ll be able to review and approve everything before sharing it with your guests.
23. What makes FloWWeds different from traditional invitations?
FloWWeds creates an emotional digital experience guests interact with and remember, rather than a traditional invitation they simply read once.
24. Can we use FloWWeds after the wedding too?
Yes. Your website will remain available for at least one year after your wedding at no additional cost. We will cover all hosting and maintenance expenses during this period. You can continue enjoying your wedding memories and guest content long after your celebration has ended.
25. Is FloWWeds suitable for destination weddings?
Absolutely. FloWWeds is especially helpful for destination weddings because guests can easily access travel details, schedules, maps, and updates in one place.
26. Is FloWWeds available internationally?
Yes. Your wedding experience can be shared worldwide instantly through a single private link.
27. What if we need help during setup?
We guide you through the entire process and make sure everything looks beautiful and works smoothly — even if you’ve never used a wedding website before.
28. How do we get started?
Simply place your order and send us your photos and wedding details. We’ll guide you through the rest step by step.
29. Can we use our own music?
For personal wedding galleries and memories, you’re welcome to use your own music.

However, for FloWWeds cinematic invitations, we use original sound design and custom-arranged classical music created specifically for the experience. Due to copyright and licensing restrictions, we’re unable to use commercial songs freely within cinematic invitations.
30. Can the invitation be created in different languages?
Currently, FloWWeds experiences are created in English. However, for special requests, we may be able to support additional languages depending on the project and customization needs. Additional fees may apply.
31. What if we don’t like the final result?
Before delivery, we’ll send your completed experience for approval. If everything feels right, we’ll finalize your project and prepare it for sharing.

Because every FloWWeds experience is crafted with its own cinematic direction and balanced design, we do not offer redesigns or alternate versions. However, if you genuinely feel the experience is not the right fit for you, we’ll gladly issue a full refund.
32. Can we request website custom design changes?
FloWWeds uses one carefully designed website experience focused on simplicity, beauty, and smooth functionality across all devices.

Rather than offering endless layout variations and redesign requests, we focus on creating a polished and balanced experience that consistently feels elegant, emotional, and easy for guests to use.
33. Do you offer fully custom projects?
If you’re looking for something outside of our standard experience, feel free to contact us directly. Some custom projects may be available depending on scope, timeline, and creative direction. Pricing varies by project.
34. Can I cancel my order after payment?
Yes. You may cancel your order and receive a full refund at any time before we receive your wedding details and photos and begin creating your wedding experience. Once production has started, cancellations and refunds are no longer available.
35. What will I receive immediately after payment?
Within a few hours of payment, you will receive access to your personal wedding website and control panel, allowing you to begin uploading photos, adding wedding details, and managing your guest experience. Your personalized cinematic RSVP invitation and Save the Date video announcement will be delivered once production is completed.
