Guest Memories

i
Guest Memories helps you reconnect with guests after your wedding and invite them to share their favorite moments, messages, and photos. Simply send a memory request by email or SMS, and guests can respond through their personalized memory form. Submitted memories are automatically collected and later displayed inside your Keepsake Box.

How to Use This Page

1. Select Guests

Choose the guests you would like to invite to share memories from your wedding celebration.

2. Send Email Invitations

Send personalized memory requests directly by email. Each guest receives a dedicated submission link.

3. Send SMS Invitations

Use SMS invitations to encourage fast participation and increase response rates.

4. Share Personalized Links

Every guest receives a personalized form link connected to their wedding invitation.

5. Invite Guests to Share Memories

Encourage guests to share stories, favorite moments, congratulations, and reflections from your wedding day.

6. Collect Personal Messages

Guests can submit thoughtful messages and memories through a simple online form.

7. Receive Guest Photos

Guests can upload photos that capture meaningful moments from your wedding celebration.

8. Track Invitation Delivery

Monitor invitation delivery status and identify guests who may need a follow-up reminder.

9. Memories Are Saved Automatically

Submitted messages and photos are automatically saved and later appear inside your Keepsake Box.

10. Follow Up with Guests

Send reminders to guests who have not yet shared their memories or photos.

Important Notes

i
Guest Memories is designed specifically for collecting memories after your wedding celebration.
i
Guests can submit both personal messages and photos through their personalized memory form.
i
All submitted memories are automatically saved and later organized inside your Keepsake Box.
i
Sending requests soon after the wedding usually results in the highest participation rate.

In short: invite guests to share memories, send personalized requests, collect messages and photos, and automatically gather everything inside your Keepsake Box for future enjoyment.
