Gift Registry allows you to create, organize, and manage your wedding gift list in one place. Add gifts, track reservations, manage your Honeymoon Fund, and control exactly what your guests see.
Why Gift Registry Matters
A well-organized registry helps guests choose meaningful gifts while preventing duplicate purchases.

Your guests can reserve gifts directly from the registry. This helps prevent duplicate purchases and keeps your gift list organized automatically.

Everything is managed from one central dashboard, including gifts, reservations, Honeymoon Fund contributions, and guest visibility settings.

How to Use This Page

1. Enable Registry Page

Use the visibility setting to control whether guests can access your Gift Registry page.

2. Set Up Your Honeymoon Fund

Add your PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle information to allow guests to contribute toward your honeymoon.

3. Choose a Gift Category

Organize gifts into categories to make browsing easier for your guests.

4. Add a New Gift

Enter the gift name, price, quantity, and any other information you want guests to see.

5. Add a Store Link

Include a shopping link so guests can quickly purchase the gift from your preferred retailer.

6. Save Your Gift

Save each gift to make it available in your registry and visible to guests.

7. Manage Your Gift List

Review all gifts, quantities, prices, links, and reservation activity from one location.

8. Review Guest Reservations

See which guests have reserved gifts and monitor reservation activity in real time.

9. Remove Reservations If Needed

Manually remove reservations when changes are required or a reservation was created by mistake.

10. Edit Existing Gifts

Update gift information whenever prices, quantities, links, or descriptions change.

11. Delete Gifts

Remove gifts that are no longer needed or available.

12. Preview Your Guest Registry

Preview the page exactly as guests will see it before sharing it with friends and family.

13. Send Registry Updates

Notify guests when gifts are added, updated, or when new registry information becomes available.

Important Notes

i
Guests can reserve gifts directly from the guest-facing registry page.
i
Reservations help prevent duplicate gift purchases.
i
Honeymoon Fund information is displayed to guests exactly as entered.
i
Hidden Gift Registry pages are not visible to guests.
i
Always preview your registry before sharing it with guests.

In short: use Gift Registry to manage gifts, Honeymoon Fund contributions, guest reservations, and the overall gift experience for your wedding guests from one central dashboard.
