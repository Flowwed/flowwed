Welcome to your Gift Registry. This page lets you create and manage your wedding wish list — so your guests always know what will truly make you happy. Everything you add here becomes part of your public wedding website.

How This Page Works

Your Gift Registry is simple, elegant, and fully under your control. Add gifts, organize them by category, edit them at any time, and keep your list perfectly up to date.

Choose a Category
Start by selecting a category. This helps guests browse your list easily.
Add Gifts
Enter the gift name, price, quantity, and a store link. Each gift appears instantly in your list.
Edit Anytime
Click the pencil icon to update any gift. Changes are saved immediately.
Remove Items
Delete gifts you no longer need. Your list always stays clean and relevant.
Store Links
Add a shop link so guests can quickly find and purchase the gift.
For Your Guests
Your registry appears beautifully on your wedding website, making gift-giving easy and joyful.

Every change is saved automatically. You can update your registry at any time, even minutes before sharing your site.

Gift Registry is designed to be graceful and stress-free — so your guests feel inspired, and you receive gifts you truly love.
