Web Video

i
The Web Video page allows you to upload, replace, and manage the main video displayed on your wedding website landing page. Your Save the Date and RSVP videos will automatically appear here once completed. You can replace them with your own video anytime.

How to Use This Page

1. Choose Your Video

Click Choose Video and select the MP4 file you would like to display on your wedding website.

2. Preview Before Uploading

Use the preview window to review your selected video before uploading it to your website.

3. Upload Your Video

Click Upload Video to publish the video to your wedding website landing page.

4. Track Upload Progress

Watch the progress bar while your video uploads to the website.

5. Replace an Existing Video

Upload a new video at any time if you'd like to update or change your landing page experience.

6. Delete a Video

Use Delete Video to remove the currently published video from your website.

7. Preview Your Website

Click Preview Your Page to see exactly how visitors will experience your landing page.

8. Keep Your Video Updated

Return anytime to upload a new video, refresh your content, or improve your website presentation.

Important Notes

i
Only MP4 videos up to 80 MB are supported.
i
The uploaded video becomes the main video displayed on your wedding website landing page. Your Save the Date and RSVP videos will automatically appear here once completed.
i
You can replace or delete your video at any time.
i
Always preview your page after uploading to confirm everything looks correct.
