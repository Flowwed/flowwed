Web Video

i
The Web Video page allows you to upload, replace, and manage the main video displayed on your wedding website landing page. Your Save the Date and RSVP videos will automatically appear here once completed. In the meantime, feel free to upload your own video and replace preloaded content while we prepare your personalized Save the Date and RSVP announcements.

How to Use This Page

1. Choose Your Video

A sample video is currently displayed on your page. To replace it, click the Choose Video button and select an MP4 file from your computer or mobile device.

2. Upload Your Video

Click Upload Video to add MP4 video file from your computer or mobile device to your wedding website's landing page. There is no Save button — your video will be published automatically as soon as the upload is complete.

3. Replace an Existing Video

Return anytime to upload a new video if you'd like to update or change your landing page experience.

4. Delete a Video

Use Delete Video to remove the currently published video from your website.

5. Preview Your Website

Click Preview Your Page to see exactly how visitors will experience your landing page.

6. Send Updates

To notify your guests about your new landing page video, use the Update button to open the guest communication tool.
