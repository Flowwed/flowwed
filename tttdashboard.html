Welcome to your Wedding Manager. This is your private control center for everything related to your wedding — guests, invitations, planning tools, and post-wedding memories. From here, you can manage every major part of your celebration in one place.

How to Use This Page

Each large ruby button opens a dedicated tool in a new tab. Hover over any button to see a short hint about what it does.

Guest Hub
Add, edit, and organize your guests. Track who is invited, attending, or still undecided.
Save The Date
Choose a template and send your Save-the-Date messages by email & SMS instantly. Collect responses automatically.
RSVP
Choose a template and send RSVP or invitation messages by email & SMS. Collect responses automatically.
Seating Chart
Arrange your guests into tables and design the perfect seating plan.
Gift Registry
Add your gift wishes and share them with your guests.
Budget
Track expenses and keep your wedding finances under control.
Wed Reports
View helpful reports about your wedding activity and engagement.
Post-Wedding
Manage everything after the big day — messages, memories, and next steps.

All your changes are saved automatically.

This dashboard is designed to keep your entire wedding organized, calm, and beautiful — so you can focus on what really matters.
