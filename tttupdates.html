Updates is your wedding news feed. Here you can post messages, changes, and announcements for your guests.

Use this page to keep everyone informed — from schedule changes to exciting news before and after the big day.

How to Use Updates

Write Your Message
Type or format your update in the editor.
Add a Photo
Upload an image to highlight your update.
Save Changes
Store your message safely.
Preview Page
See how guests will read it.
Notify Guests
Send your update via email & SMS.

Why It Matters

Updates keep everyone in sync. They reduce confusion, build excitement, and make your guests feel involved in every step of your wedding journey.
