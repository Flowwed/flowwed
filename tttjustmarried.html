Gallery

i
Gallery helps you create and manage a beautiful wedding photo album for your guests. Upload photos, add short notes, include background music, preview the guest page, and control whether the album is visible on your wedding website.

How to Use This Page

1. Enable the Album Page

Use Enable Album Webpage to show or hide your Gallery page from guests. Keep it hidden while building the album if needed.

2. Control Floating Tips

Use Enable Floating Tips to show or hide helpful floating tips on the guest-facing gallery page.

3. Choose a Photo Slot

Select the photo number before uploading. Each image is placed into a specific gallery slot.

4. Upload Photos

Choose an image file and click Upload Photo to Your Page to add it to the wedding gallery.

5. Track Upload Progress

The progress bar shows the upload status while your photo is being added to the gallery.

6. Add a Photo Note

After uploading, you can add a short note or caption to explain the moment behind the photo.

7. Review Uploaded Photos

The gallery panel displays uploaded photos with their notes, making it easy to review what guests will see.

8. Delete Photos

Remove any photo from the gallery if you want to replace it or keep the album clean and organized.

9. Add Background Music

Upload an MP3 file to add background music to the gallery page and make the guest experience more personal.

10. Preview Music

Use the audio preview to check the selected music before or after uploading it to the page.

11. Preview Guest View

Click Preview Your Page to see how your Gallery will appear to guests on your wedding website.

12. Send Guest Updates

Use the update option to notify guests when new photos or music have been added to the gallery.

Important Notes

i
You can upload up to 125 photos to your Gallery.
i
Each photo must be under 5MB.
i
Photo notes are limited to short captions, so keep them simple and meaningful.
i
Background music must be an MP3 file.
i
Always preview the Gallery page after uploading photos or music to confirm the guest experience looks correct.

In short: use Gallery to upload wedding photos, add captions, manage your album, include optional music, preview the guest page, and create a beautiful visual experience for everyone visiting your wedding website.
