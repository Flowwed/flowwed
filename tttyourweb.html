Welcome to your Web Manager. This page is the control center for your wedding website. From here, you can edit every section of your site, update text, upload photos, and instantly preview how everything looks.

Main Sections

Each large button opens an editor for a specific part of your site:

Our Story – Write and update your love story.
Wedding Party – Add or edit bridesmaids, groomsmen, and roles.
Wedding Info – Edit ceremony details, schedule, and important notes.
Venue & Map – Set your venue and show guests where to go.
Travel Info – Share hotel, transport, and travel tips.
Gift Registry – Add links or instructions for gifts.
Photo Gallery – Upload and manage photos.
Just Married – Post content for after the wedding.
Updates – Send announcements or changes to your guests.

Click any button to open that section in a new tab.
