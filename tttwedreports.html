RSVP Reports

i
The RSVP Reports page gives you a live overview of guest responses. You can track attendance, review and update guest groups, view meal selections and guest messages, send reminders, export reports, and manage follow-ups — all from one place.

How to Use This Page

1. View RSVP Statistics

The ruby summary cards at the top show your live RSVP results: Yes, No, Maybe, and No Response. These numbers help you quickly understand the current status of your guest list.

2. RSVP No Response

The No Response group is a live copy of your Guest Hub guest list. As guests respond to your RSVP form, they are automatically moved to the appropriate response groups.

3. Open Guest Groups

Click any summary card to open the matching guest group below. For example, clicking RSVP Yes displays only the guests who confirmed attendance.

4. Review Guest Details

Each table shows the selected group with guest names, emails, number of guests, meal choices, and messages. This gives you a clear operational view of each RSVP group.

5. Read Guest Messages

Guest messages and meal selections submitted with RSVPs appear in the table. Use Show to view longer messages and Hide to collapse them again.

6. Send Email Reminders

Select guests and click Send Email. The built-in email composer allows you to send reminders and wedding-related announcements directly from this page.

7. Send SMS Notifications

Click Send SMS to generate a QR code. Scan it with your phone to quickly send a reminder to the selected guest or RSVP group.

8. Follow Up with Guests

The Maybe and No Response groups are especially useful for follow-ups. Use them to remind guests to confirm their final attendance before your RSVP deadline.

9. Move Guests Between Groups

If a guest changes their RSVP response after it has been submitted, use Move Guests to Another Group to place them in the correct RSVP category.

10. Share with Vendors

Download your guest list as a CSV file for printing or easy sharing with planners, vendors, and other wedding stakeholders using your preferred email service, such as Gmail, Yahoo, or Outlook.

Important Notes

i
RSVP Reports updates automatically when guests submit their RSVP forms, so your guest statistics and group lists stay current.
i
The RSVP No Response group is one of the most important sections. It helps you quickly identify guests who have not yet responded and may need a reminder by email or SMS.
i
Guests sometimes change their plans. If a guest updates their decision after submitting an RSVP, use Move Guests to Another Group to place them in the correct RSVP category and keep your attendance records accurate.
i
In rare cases, heavy server traffic may cause slower page loading. If a page takes longer than a minute to load, simply refresh it and continue your work.
