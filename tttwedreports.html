The Wed Reports page is your live command center for guest data. It transforms every response into clear statistics, instant lists, and ready-to-use communication groups.

Instead of guessing who replied, who didn’t, and who is still undecided, you see everything in real time — organized, visual, and actionable.

How Wed Reports Works

Glass Summary Cards
Each ruby card shows a live count: Save-the-Date and RSVP — Yes, No, Maybe, No Response.
Tap to Explore
Click any card to instantly open the matching guest list below.
Live Guest Tables
Each table shows names, emails, and phones for that exact group.
Message Groups
Select guests and send Email & SMS directly from this page.
Export & Print
Download any group as CSV — perfect for planners and vendors.
Delivery Feedback
Failed messages are highlighted so nothing slips through.

Why This Matters

Wed Reports turns raw guest data into decisions: who to remind, who confirmed, who is silent, and how many people you’re truly planning for.

You always know:
– how many guests are coming
– who needs a reminder
– which group to message
– what to export for vendors

It removes guesswork and replaces it with clarity. Every number becomes action.
